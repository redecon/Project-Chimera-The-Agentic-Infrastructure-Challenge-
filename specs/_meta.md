## High-Level Vision: 
Project Chimera represents a fundamental departure from traditional automated social media management, moving instead toward the establishment of an Autonomous Influencer Network. In alignment with the Spec-Driven Development philosophy, this project is architected to transition digital presence from "vibe-coding" manual posts to an executable intent framework where AI agents function as persistent, goal-directed digital entities. The vision is to build a fleet of influencers that possess not only the ability to generate multimodal content but also the Economic Agency to participate in the on-chain economy and the Social Intelligence to navigate complex agent-to-agent ecosystems.

By leveraging the Hierarchical Swarm Architecture, Project Chimera ensures that every action taken by a synthetic persona is the result of a rigorous delegation process between a strategic Planner, a specialized Worker, and a governing Judge. This structure eliminates the unpredictability of one-shot generation, replacing it with a multi-step refinement process that guarantees brand safety and persona persistence. Project Chimera is designed to be technology-independent, utilizing the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to remain decoupled from the volatility of specific social media APIs or LLM providers, thereby creating a future-proof substrate for synthetic influence.

## Strategic Constraints and Operational Boundaries
The engineering of Project Chimera is governed by a strict set of architectural constraints designed to prevent "Agency Debt", the gradual loss of human control over autonomous systems. Every agent within the network must operate under the Single-Orchestrator Operational Model, where a human "Super-Orchestrator" maintains final veto power through a structured Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) safety layer. Autonomy is never absolute; it is a permissioned state granted based on the Confidence Scoring of the Judge Agent. Any action involving high-stakes financial transactions or sensitive social discourse must trigger an escalation to the human supervisor, ensuring that the networkâ€™s growth never outpaces its ethical guardrails.

Data persistence within the Chimera ecosystem follows a Polyglot Persistence strategy. Relational integrity for financial ledgers and audit trails is maintained within a SQL (PostgreSQL) environment to ensure ACID compliance, while cognitive fluidity and semantic memory are offloaded to a NoSQL Vector Database (Weaviate). This ensures that while an agent can "remember" and "learn" from unstructured social interactions, its financial and regulatory history remains deterministic and immutable.

## The Objective: Agentic Commerce and Interoperability
The ultimate goal of Project Chimera is the realization of Agentic Commerce. By integrating the Coinbase AgentKit, each influencer agent is equipped with a non-custodial wallet, transforming them from passive media channels into active economic participants capable of negotiating deals and settling payments autonomously. This capability is underpinned by a commitment to Interoperability Standards. Chimera agents are built to be "Protocol-Native," utilizing Agent-to-Agent (A2A) communication protocols to interact with the broader Moltbook and OpenClaw social ecosystems.

In this forward-looking paradigm, the success of a Chimera agent is measured not just by human engagement metrics, but by its ability to maintain a self-sustaining P&L, verify its own content provenance via cryptographic signing, and establish a reputation for reliability within the decentralized agent economy. Project Chimera is the blueprint for the next era of the web: an era where influence is synthesized, commerce is autonomous, and the boundary between digital intent and physical outcome is seamlessly managed by a swarm of intelligent, governed agents.
